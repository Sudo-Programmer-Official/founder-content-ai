# FounderContent AI Roadmap

## Roadmap Principle

Build FounderContent AI in phases that keep the product narrow early and expand only after the core workflow proves useful.

The long-term product can become a Founder Personal Branding OS, but the roadmap should begin with one reliable founder content workflow.

## Phase 1: MVP

### Goal

Launch the smallest version of FounderContent AI that gives a founder immediate value.

### Product Focus

**Idea -> Hook -> Post**

### Deliverables

- homepage with clear founder-focused positioning
- `/founder-content-ideas`
- `/linkedin-hook-generator`
- `/linkedin-post-generator`
- Express API endpoints for idea, hook, and post generation
- shared prompt definitions for the three MVP generators
- copy-ready output flows

### Success Condition

A founder can move from topic discovery to a publishable LinkedIn draft in one session.

### Explicit Exclusions

- scheduling
- analytics dashboards
- social integrations
- team workflows
- multi-platform generation

## Phase 2: SEO Engine

### Goal

Create a repeatable search acquisition system that brings founders into the product.

### Deliverables

- reusable SEO page template
- `/linkedin-post-ideas-for-founders`
- `/build-in-public-post-ideas`
- `/founder-storytelling-examples`
- internal links from SEO pages into generator routes
- basic metadata and on-page SEO structure

### Success Condition

Organic visitors can discover the site through founder-content searches and enter the generator workflow from landing pages.

### Explicit Exclusions

- broad blog publishing strategy
- large-scale programmatic SEO expansion
- non-founder traffic segments

## Phase 3: Content Library

### Goal

Turn successful patterns into reusable content assets.

### Deliverables

- curated library of founder post examples
- reusable templates and post structures
- saved prompt presets or categorized writing angles
- stronger internal linking between templates and generators

### Success Condition

Users can browse proven founder content formats, adapt them quickly, and reuse them as part of their writing process.

### Explicit Exclusions

- performance dashboards
- automated recommendations based on engagement data

## Phase 4: Analytics

### Goal

Help founders understand what content performs better and improve future posts.

### Deliverables

- post analyzer or post scoring workflows
- engagement-aware feedback concepts
- basic content performance signals
- recommendations for improving future posts

### Success Condition

The product moves from content generation into content improvement using simple analytics and feedback loops.

### Explicit Exclusions

- full social media management suite
- broad multi-channel analytics platform

## Roadmap Notes

### Deferred Vision

These ideas remain part of the broader vision but are intentionally deferred:

- direct publishing
- scheduling
- multi-workspace management
- multi-platform content repurposing
- automation pipelines

### Why This Sequence

This roadmap creates leverage in the right order:

1. prove the founder workflow
2. acquire traffic through high-intent SEO
3. build reusable content assets
4. add improvement loops through analytics

That sequence keeps the product understandable and reduces the risk of building a broad platform before the core use case works.
